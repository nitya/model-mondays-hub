Model Mondays is back for **Season 4**, kicking off **Monday, August 10** with
12 weekly episodes running through early November — with a break the week of
**September 7 for Labor Day**. As always, the series pitch holds: *as an AI
native engineer, your journey starts with model choice. Build your Model IQ
one week at a time.*

Each week mixes a rotating set of segment formats: **Tech Spotlights**,
**Customer Stories**, **Study Corner**, and **Partner Spotlights** — giving you
a steady drumbeat of hands-on demos and news from across the Microsoft
Foundry model catalog and partner ecosystem.

## Recently on Model Mondays (Season 3 recap)

Before Season 4 starts, here are four sessions worth catching up on:

- **[Unlocking Enterprise Value with Cohere](/episodes/cohere-enterprise-value/)**
  — a Partner Spotlight on Cohere's Command models, embeddings, re-rank, and
  the new Cohere Transcribe ASR model.
- **[Build AI with Hugging Face Models, Build Enterprise Agents With Agent 365 & Work IQ](/episodes/hugging-face-agent-365-work-iq/)**
  — Jeff Boudier (Hugging Face) on deploying open models securely, plus
  Srikumar Nair on Agent 365 and Work IQ MCP servers.
- **[Open Models with Fireworks AI & Microsoft Foundry MCP Server](/episodes/fireworks-foundry-mcp-server/)**
  — Shaunak Godbole (Fireworks AI) on scaling open models, and a first look
  at the Foundry MCP Server.
- **[Building Microsoft Foundry Agents Within VS Code](/episodes/building-foundry-agents-vscode/)**
  — the Season 3 finale, covering the Foundry Toolkit VS Code extension and
  Foundry Skills' observability capabilities.
